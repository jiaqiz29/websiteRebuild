Bio Blurb

JIAQI is a member of the U15A Rugby Team at Guildford Royal Grammar School. He started the season at 14, right-side winger but was seen fit as an outside centre, later on in the early mid-season by the new head coach, number 13, and is an undeniable starter for the team.

He only comes off when hes injure - which he has this season twice, with first a cut head, then a swollen knee though he played through the swollen knee and came up with a very physical performance against St Johns Leatherhead.

He has scored 2 tries this season and created countless assists and huge tackles for the team.

During the 2022-2023 season, RGS went on bumpy 4-0-7 journey through out 11 games, scoring a total of 168 points(avg.15.3PTS/GAME), against tough opponents, with each their unique playing style, strength and weaknesses which has given the entire squad plenty of experiences for the years to come.

Mentions to Captain: Harry Chilcott; Pack Leader: Max Webb; Backs Leader: Will Mellor; Vice Cap: Luke-M-Hicks; Butter Fingered: Ollie Crawford; Bulker: Kai Hobbs; Monster: Ben Avery; Creep Athlete: Bajo Loye; Scoliosis: Zack Grover; Crippled: Tommy Mucmurray (who didn't play a single game) for carrying the team through what was a rough but enjoyable season.

And now all the wait is for the Hockey season, which he would probably feature in the U15B team and also the Rugby 7s fixtures simultaneously.
